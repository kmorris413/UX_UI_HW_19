My UX Skills

Persona Development

By immersing myself in user research I am able to create very strong User Persona's that are equally relatable and unique. This results in an effective storytelling experience that can be incorporated throughout the entire design process.

Journey Map Creation

My goal when creating a Journey Map is to clearly depict a user's high and low points while navigating through a product/feature. I do this by emphasizing the user's emotions, feelings and expectations.

User Experience Interviewing

My main objective when interviewing is to gain as many useful insights as possible. I find great success by having more of an open conversation because this allows users to relax and share more information with me.
